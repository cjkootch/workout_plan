PRINCIPLES
Nutrition · Progression · TRT · Recovery · Supplements
Not medical advice. TRT notes are programming context only. All hormone adjustments must be cleared by your physician.
Weekly Schedule
▼
  • Monday — Push (Chest / Shoulders / Tris)
  • Tuesday — Pull (Back / Bis)
  • Wednesday — Rest or light walk
  • Thursday — Legs
  • Friday — Upper Power (Arms / Shoulders / Core)
  • Saturday — Athletic / Conditioning
  • Sunday — Full rest. Sleep and food.
Nutrition Strategy
▼

Eating intuitively works if you anchor it to one rule: protein first, every meal.

  • Target 235–250g protein daily (1g+ per lb bodyweight)
  • Caloric target: 3,200–3,600 kcal/day lean bulk
  • Carbs around training sessions — pre and post window
  • Don't fear fats — testosterone production depends on them
  • Minimum 1 gallon water/day on training days
Progression Model
▼

Double progression: every exercise tracks both reps and load.

  • Hit top of rep range → add weight next session
  • Can't hit bottom of range → reduce weight 5–10%
  • Compounds: aim +5 lbs/week minimum
  • Isolation: progress every 1–2 weeks
  • Deload every 6th week: drop volume 40%, keep intensity
  • Log every session. Memory is not a training log.
TRT & Hormone Context
▼

At 100–150mg/wk you're in the upper physiological range. Recovery and protein synthesis are already enhanced above natural baseline.

  • Current dose: sufficient for serious lean mass gains
  • Dose increase: only if stalled after 8+ weeks of consistent training + diet
  • Labs before any increase: Total T, Free T, E2, Hematocrit, PSA
  • Higher doses increase E2 — discuss AI management with physician
  • Sleep is the primary anabolic driver on TRT — protect it
Achilles Management
▼
  • Avoid: box jumps, sprint accelerations, heavy calf raises
  • Safe: bike, rower, controlled squats, leg press, hip thrust
  • Any sharp tendon pain = stop that movement immediately
  • Eccentric heel drops daily: 3×15 off a step at 50% intensity
  • Target full clearance by week 8 — reassess running then
Recovery Protocol
▼
  • Sleep minimum 7.5 hrs — non-negotiable on TRT
  • Active recovery: walking, stretching, sauna if available
  • Post-session: 40–50g protein within 60 min
  • Magnesium glycinate 400mg before bed
  • Omega-3 3g EPA/DHA — joint health and Achilles recovery
